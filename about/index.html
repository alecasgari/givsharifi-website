Biography

A neurosurgeon patients search as Giv and Guive

Prof. Giv Sharifi — frequently searched and known online as Dr Guive Sharifi or Guive Sharifi — is a board-certified neurosurgeon practising in the UAE and Iran. Whether you find him as Giv or Guive, you are looking at the same surgeon: a clinician and academic dedicated to precise, patient-centred neurosurgical care.

He holds an Iranian Board of Neurosurgery and a DHA license in Dubai, and serves as Professor of Neurosurgery at Shahid Beheshti University of Medical Sciences. His practice spans complex cranial surgery, spinal neurosurgery, and endoscopic pituitary procedures for local and international patients.

With more than 25 years of experience and over 28,000 neurosurgical procedures, Prof. Sharifi combines operating-theatre skill with a substantial research record — 240+ peer-reviewed publications that keep clinical decisions grounded in evidence.
