What topics does this neurosurgery blog cover?

Articles cover brain surgery, spinal surgery, pituitary tumours, minimally invasive techniques, robotic and AI-assisted surgery, pediatric neurosurgery, spinal cord injuries, and recovery guidance — written for patients and families.

Are these articles written by a qualified neurosurgeon?

Yes. All content is written or clinically reviewed by Prof. Giv Sharifi, a board-certified neurosurgeon with 25+ years of surgical experience in Dubai and Tehran.

Absolutely. or contact us via WhatsApp. Bring your MRI scans for a specialist review.

These articles are for educational purposes only and do not replace a personal consultation with a qualified neurosurgeon. Every patient's condition is unique — a specialist review of your imaging is essential before any treatment decision.
