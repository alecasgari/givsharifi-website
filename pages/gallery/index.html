About This Neurosurgery Photo Gallery

This gallery offers a transparent look at the environments where complex cranial and spinal procedures are planned and performed. Images include the operating theatre during neurosurgical cases, consultation rooms at partner hospitals, MRI and diagnostic review with patients, multidisciplinary surgical teams, and academic congresses in Tehran and internationally.

Prof. Giv Sharifi is a fellowship-trained neurosurgeon with more than 25 years of experience in brain tumour surgery, spine surgery, endoscopic pituitary and skull base surgery, and minimally invasive techniques. Patients travel from the UAE, Gulf region, Europe, and beyond for second opinions, elective surgery, and medical tourism to Iran with full coordination.

Brain & spine conditions in our photography

Many images relate to common conditions patients search for when choosing a neurosurgeon:

  • Brain tumour resection — glioma, meningioma, metastasis, and skull base lesions
  • Minimally invasive and endoscopic pituitary surgery for pituitary adenoma
  • Spinal decompression, microdiscectomy, fusion, and treatment of herniated disc or spinal stenosis
  • Vascular and skull base neurosurgical procedures
  • Post-operative recovery, imaging follow-up, and multidisciplinary tumour boards

Neurosurgeon in Dubai & Tehran

Prof. Sharifi combines academic excellence — including 260+ peer-reviewed publications — with hands-on surgical skill in both cranial and spinal neurosurgery. Whether you need treatment for a brain tumour, spinal disorder, pituitary condition, or a second opinion on a complex case, you can request a free consultation online or in person in Dubai or Tehran.

International patients from Dubai, Abu Dhabi, Oman, Kuwait, Iraq, and Europe can arrange imaging review, travel, and hospital admission through our medical tourism coordination service.
