Neurosurgery Photo Gallery — Prof. Giv Sharifi

Photographs from the clinic, operating theatre, and patient care journey with Prof. Giv Sharifi — board-certified neurosurgeon serving patients in Dubai, Tehran, and internationally.

Inside a Modern Neurosurgery Practice

This gallery offers a transparent look at the environments where brain surgery, spinal surgery, and complex cranial procedures are planned and performed. Images include the operating theatre during neurosurgical cases, consultation rooms at partner hospitals, MRI and diagnostic review with patients, and the multidisciplinary teams that support safe, precise surgery.

Prof. Giv Sharifi is a fellowship-trained neurosurgeon with more than 25 years of experience in brain tumor surgery, spine surgery, endoscopic pituitary and skull base surgery, and minimally invasive techniques. Patients travel from the UAE, Gulf region, Europe, and beyond for second opinions, elective surgery, and medical tourism to Iran with full coordination.

What you will see in this gallery

  • Surgery — operating theatre photography during brain and spine procedures, surgical microscopes, neuronavigation, and team coordination in sterile conditions.
  • Diagnostics — review of brain MRI, spinal imaging, and pre-operative planning with patients and families.
  • Clinic & patient care — consultations at Tandis Hospital, Farda Hospital, and private clinic settings in Dubai and Tehran.
  • Team — neurosurgical nurses, anaesthesia colleagues, and support staff who make complex surgery possible.
  • Events — congresses, symposia, and academic meetings in Tehran and internationally.

Expert Neurosurgery in Dubai & Tehran

Choosing a neurosurgeon is one of the most important healthcare decisions you will make. Prof. Sharifi combines academic excellence — including 240+ peer-reviewed publications — with hands-on surgical skill in both cranial and spinal neurosurgery. Whether you need treatment for a brain tumor (glioma, meningioma, metastasis), herniated disc or spinal stenosis, pituitary adenoma, or a second opinion on a complex case, you can request a free consultation online or in person.

  • Minimally invasive and endoscopic pituitary surgery
